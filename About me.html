About Me

Hi, Meet the real marketing nerd!

I believe that understanding people’s behavior and studying how they will react
to certain things is one of the interesting aspects of decision-making from a
psychological perspective. And here I can just add one of Peter Drucker’s quotes.

“The aim of marketing is to know and understand the customer so well that the
product or service fits him and sells itself.” – Peter Drucker.

I studied in Tabeetha school and got my GCSE O and A level. That is the place
where I determined what I want to be. So that, thanks to my work ethic and
dedication, I could achieve my goal! Besides that, I also have a Network
Engineering MCSE certification.

However, knowledge is power and guided by this approach, I decided to keep
pushing forward. I use every possibility to educate myself and improve my skills.
I passed a lot of online courses related to business development, digital
marketing, social media marketing and sales. Being an active member of affLift
forum and Warrior Forum, I always stay tuned for updates and innovations in the
world of marketing. I am motivated and inspired by following such marketing gurus
as Neil Patel, Barry Schwartz, Eric Sui.

Above all, I speak three different languages–English, Arabic, and Hebrew, and
thanks to these languages, I can connect and cooperate with people from
all around the world.
